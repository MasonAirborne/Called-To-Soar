Wings in the Making

From fueling jets to logging my first solo, I’m building the discipline, judgment, and hours it takes to earn my seat up front. I didn’t start in aviation — but every step brought me here.

Read more

At 22, I sat in a cockpit for the first time. At 26, I decided to go all‑in. I’m a first‑gen college grad who finished debt‑free through 25+ scholarships and relentless hustle. Today I’m hands‑on in corporate aviation — fueling, marshaling, battling weather — learning how the industry really works. Every time I watch a pilot (especially a woman) step off a Pilatus, King Air, or Gulfstream, it reignites the same truth: this is where I’m headed.
