About Me

I’m Mason Airborne — a determined, full-of-life adventurer with a love for flight, people, and the thrill of the unknown. My mission is to inspire others to chase the dreams that scare them, and to prove that the leap of faith is worth it.

About Me

"Why NOT me?" — That’s the question I live by.

I’ve never been the type to sit still or play it safe. From catching waves on a surfboard to fueling jets on the ramp, I’ve always chased that heart-racing mix of awe and calm that comes from embracing the unknown.

I didn’t step into a cockpit until I was 22 — but when I did, I felt the same rush I used to get from the ocean, only this time, it was God’s creation stretched out beneath me.

I worked my way through college debt-free, juggling multiple jobs and supporting my family along the way. Basketball taught me grit, leadership, and dedication; life taught me resilience, hustle, and faith.

Now, my dream is to become a corporate pilot — flying sleek jets across the skies, inspiring the underdog, and proving that no dream is too bold if you’re willing to put in the work.

If I can make one person believe in themselves — or even just smile in a day — I’ve succeeded.

Goals

  • Earn IFR, Commercial, Multi-Engine, and CFI ratings.
  • Pursue a career in corporate aviation.
  • Inspire others to invest in themselves and chase the dreams they’ve been afraid to start.
  • Be a role model for the next generation of pilots, especially those who feel like they don’t fit the mold.
