About Me

A quick intro—why I’m “Called 2 Soar.”

I’m Mason, a first-gen college grad turned pilot-in-training, learning aviation from the ramp to the cockpit. My journey didn’t start in a straight line, but every step gave me grit, faith, and focus.

After years of regular jobs, I committed to flight training and working at the airport—so I could learn the industry from the ground up. I believe in excellence, safety, and showing others what’s possible through hard work and grace. Why not me?
