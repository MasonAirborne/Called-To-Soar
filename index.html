About Me

I’m Lindsey Mehana — a determined, full-of-life adventurer with a love for flight, people, and the thrill of the unknown. My mission is to inspire others to chase the dreams that scare them, and to prove that the leap of faith is worth it.

“Why NOT me?” — That’s the question I live by.

I didn’t step into a cockpit until I was 22. I worked my way through college debt-free and discovered the same rush in a cockpit that I used to get from the ocean — awe, focus, and joy.

Now, my dream is to become a corporate pilot — flying sleek jets across the skies, inspiring the underdog, and proving that no dream is too bold if you’re willing to put in the work.
