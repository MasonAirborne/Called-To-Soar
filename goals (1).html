Flight Goals & Funding

My dream is to become a professional corporate pilot—flying King Airs, Pilatus PC-24s, and Gulfstreams across the country and globe. But dreams like this require more than passion — they require funding, commitment, and faith.

I’ve already saved over $20,000 toward training and work full-time at a Class C airport to stay immersed in aviation. But advanced ratings like my Instrument, Commercial, and Multi-Engine still cost tens of thousands more. My total financial goal is approximately $60,000.

I’ve applied to several aviation scholarships including:

  • Women in Aviation International
  • Women in Corporate Aviation
  • The Part-Time Pilot Flight Scholarship

Every bit of support counts — financially, emotionally, or spiritually.
