My Flight Training Goals

My dream is to become a professional corporate pilot — flying jets like the Gulfstream, Bombardier, or Dassault across the skies. It’s not just about the aircraft — it’s about reaching people, building a legacy, and breaking barriers in aviation.

I'm currently working full-time at a busy Class C airport, gaining hands-on experience in operations and fueling my passion for aviation daily. I’ve already saved over Thousands of Dollars toward flight school and recently completed my first solo flight — a milestone that marked the beginning of my path to the skies.

Becoming a pilot requires more than passion — it takes funding, focus, and perseverance. I am in works to complete the rest of my ratings (Instrument, Commercial, Multi-Engine)

I'm actively applying for aviation scholarships, including:

  • Women in Aviation International (WAI)
  • Women in Corporate Aviation
  • The Part-Time Pilot Flight Scholarship

Each application, each flight hour, and each shift at the airport brings me closer to inspiring others to follow their dreams — especially young women and underrepresented aspiring aviators.

Your support — whether through encouragement, sharing my story, or financial assistance — lifts my wings and fuels this mission.
