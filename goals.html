My Flight Training Goals

My dream is to become a professional corporate pilot — flying jets like the Gulfstream, Bombardier, or Dassault. It’s not just about the aircraft — it’s about reaching people, building a legacy, and breaking barriers in aviation.

I’m working full-time at a busy Class C airport, gaining hands-on experience in operations and fueling my passion daily. I’ve saved thousands of dollars toward training and recently completed my first solo — a milestone that marked the beginning of this climb.

Becoming a pilot takes more than passion — it takes funding, focus, and perseverance. I’m working to complete the rest of my ratings: Instrument, Commercial, and Multi-Engine.

I’m actively applying for aviation scholarships, including:

  • Women in Aviation International (WAI)
  • Women in Corporate Aviation
  • The Part-Time Pilot Flight Scholarship

Every application, flight hour, and shift at the airport brings me closer to inspiring others to chase their dreams — especially young women and under-represented aspiring aviators.

Your support — encouragement, sharing my story, or financial assistance — lifts my wings and fuels this mission.
